Mythic Craft

Single-file HTML5 voxel sandbox with streaming Perlin terrain and mythic procedural landmarks.

  • WASD move, Mouse look, Space jump, Shift sprint, Esc unlock cursor
  • Left click break block, Right click place block
  • Scroll or press number keys (1-7) to select block
  • Explore floating temples, obsidian fortresses, crystal ruins, and world-tree sanctums
Landmarks are procedurally generated in all directions. Walk farther to discover more structures in the infinite world.
